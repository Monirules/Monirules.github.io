    Benchmark Datasets

    1. Human Activity Recognition from Wearable Sensors

    A comprehensive dataset of 72,000 readings of 6 persons, collected using a 3D accelerometer and gyroscope sensor from Rasperry PI 4 Device. We also proposed a mobile app to collect and tracking human activity.

    Dataset Link: (Coming soon)

    2. Diabetic Foot Ulcer Dataset

    A specialized image dataset comprising 500 foot images (250 normal, 250 with ulcers) for the detection of Diabetic Foot Ulcers. This dataset was collected with approval from the North South University IRB/ERC (Application No. 2024/OR-NSU/IRB/1110).

    Dataset Link: Click Here

    3. DeepFake and Real Face Image Collection

    A dataset of 500 facial images, including real Bangladeshi individuals of diverse genders and ages, collected with explicit written consent for deepfake detection research. Data collection was approved by North South University IRB/ERC (Application No. 2024/OR-NSU/IRB/1109).

    Dataset Link: Click Here

    4. Ground-Based Cloud Segmentation Dataset

    A segmented dataset of 110 ground-based cloud images, meticulously annotated using Roboflow. This dataset was developed for training and evaluating U-Net based Fully Convolutional Neural Networks (F-CNN) for cloud segmentation tasks.

    Dataset Link: (Coming soon)
